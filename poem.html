Happy Birthday To Me
The only one that is quite worthy,
of such praise and sorrow empathy,
that it rivals that of mark Anthony's.

A day to celebrate the birth of beauty,
the birth thereof resulted in travesty,
the day of which the clocks of destiny,
had begun to swing to imminent tragedy.

As I recall my own, existing and subsisting,
a life that was spent on pervasive brooding,
a condescending ego that was singing,
a narcissist was only ever barely clinging.

The winds of change have finally come,
the body of a child to fetch the soul therefrom,
an evil awaits to swallow it whole from,
the visage until the mind had gone numb.

There it was, the fate to which is made,
according to the image of evil, it delayed,
decomposing what was already decayed,
there it stood bare, so anxious and afraid.

Time can only move in one straight direction,
which limits the possibilities of my benediction,
which I owe to it my most conceited distinction,
to those who otherwise, were firm in conviction,

Time seeks to wither us slowly and painfully,
with each second passing, quite boringly,
something returns and remains faithfully,
to the day that reminds, the birth of tragedy.
That is, the day of birth, Happy birthday to me!
